The best movies according to Alisa

Top 3 movies of all times

Remember me (2010)

A thought-provoking and sweet love story centered around drama and tragedy. This movie left me utterly speechless for hours.

Avengers saga

Adventures, saving the world, friendship, love, great characters and plot - everything that you would expect a movie to have is there.

The beauty inside (2015)

A South-Korean rom-com about true love. It is an engaging love story about the man that changes its appearance every morning. He falls in love with a girl who proves that a true love is about the beauty inside.
